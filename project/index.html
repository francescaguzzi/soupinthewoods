✦ How to Play ✦
Collect Mushrooms
Click on the mushrooms that appear in the forest to collect them in your backpack.
Backpack
The collected mushrooms appear in the panel at the bottom. You can empty it with the "Empty bag" button.
Make the Soup
Collect at least 3 mushrooms and click on the pan to cook the soup.
See if the critters enjoy it!
The mice will come to see if the soup is tasty! Be careful, they are picky eaters!
